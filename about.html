Meet SAMZIO

SAMZIO was founded in 2017 by a group of 3 friends. It initially started as a clothing store selling non-branded, blank clothes in Ottawa. Later proceeded, in 2019, with an e-commerce website to provide deliveries all over canada. In 2020, SAMZIO launched in-store clothing customization for its customers in Ottawa. They later opened another store in Toronto in 2021. SAMZIO's goal now is to redesign their e-commerce website to support custom design and previews

There is a high demand of customized t-shirts and there exist many companies competing to offer unbeatable services to customers, but SAMZIO stands out and provide better solutions for customers.

Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to express their unique personalities through customizable clothing that is both stylish and sustainable and supply each customer with personalized and distinctive product.

Vision

Our vision is to revolutionize the fashion industry by offering a personalized and sustainable alternative to fast fashion. We aspire to inspire others to join us in our mission to create a more sustainable and inclusive future for fashion. We want to be a high-quality, reasonably priced custom brand.

What we Offer

  • We offer a wide range of customizable as well as blank clothings.
  • We offer free shipping for orders greater than $100 across Canada.
  • Customers can preview designs on chosen clothing and customise as will.
  • No minimum orders required. (Customers can order as from 1 product)
